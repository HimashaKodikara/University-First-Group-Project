Radiology



Fully equipped with the latest technology, our Radiology unit handles the imaging and diagnostics side of your medical experience with us.
As Swiss Health is at the forefront of imaging research, this department is run by qualified and experienced professionals who are experts in radiology.

Radiography
  1. Digital x-ray/Computerised x-ray
  2. Plain X-rays such as chest, abdomen and extremities
  3. Special investigations such as IVU (Intravenous Urography), Micturating cystourethrogram (MCUG), HSG and Sialogram

MRI
  1. Brain
  2. Spine
  3. Abdomen and pelvis
  4. MRCP
  5. MRI Breast
  6. MRA (Contrast enhanced/non contrast)

CT Scan
  1. Brain
  2. Neck
  3. Chest
  4. CT angiogram for coronary arteries, main arteries and peripheral arteries
  5. Abdomen and pelvis (Cologram, KUB, Etc.)

Mammography
  • Digital mammo with tomosynthesis (3D volume data assessed on work station) mammography complemented by ultrasound image of the breast.
