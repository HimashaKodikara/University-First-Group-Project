What is Physiotherapy?
Physiotherapy is treatment to restore, maintain, and make the most of a patient’s mobility, function, and well-being. Physiotherapy helps through physical rehabilitation, injury prevention, and health and fitness. Physiotherapists get you involved in your own recovery.

Hydrotherapy
Hydrotherapy is the use of water for therapy, rehabilitation, exercise,
relaxation and more. The unique properties of water, including the decreased effects of gravity,
the effects of buoyancy, hydrostatic pressure are used by the physiotherapist along with their diagnostic skills, exercise knowledge and other treatment techniques to enhance the recovery and rehabilitation of clients with a wide range of conditions and provide a unique environment to maintain health, well-being and fitness.
  • Aquatic physiotherapy
  • Water exercise classes
  • Deep water running and fitness
  • Movement in water for people with disabilities
  • Aquatic massage

Common reasons people seek Physiotherapy help.

Knee Pain Neck Pain Headaches Hip pain Back Pain Hamstrings Shoulder Pain Leg Pain
Whether you are suffering pain from injury or simply wear-and-tear, we understand the struggle it can take on your day-to-day lifestyle. Walking up stairs. Lifting heavy loads. Getting up and sitting down. These are just some of the everyday tasks that become impossible when you suffer from knee pain. Our qualified physios will assess the cause of your knee pain and provide treatment to sustain your strength. Poor posture, injury arthritis, and a sedentary lifestyle are all common contributors to neck pain. 1 in 5 Sri Lankans suffers from neck pain, so if you’re one of them, ensure you book an appointment with our team and take the first step towards a pain-free lifestyle. Tension, stress, poor posture, and prolonged use of a computer or phone can lead to painful strain on your neck, leading to headaches. Relieve tension and ease a painful headache with effective physio solutions from Asiri Physio Hip pain can strike all age groups and can be the result of groin and hamstring injuries, muscle strains, bursitis, and arthritis, just to name a few! At Asiri Physio we solve pain by getting to the root of the problem and create tailored solutions to strengthen your hips. Frustrated by ongoing back pain? Is it stopping you from living an active lifestyle? You’re not alone. In fact, most people will experience some sort of back pain during their lifetime. We prescribe routines to strengthen your back and treatments to relieve pain. Treatment to manage hamstring injuries or pain is designed to reduce inflammation and improve muscle range of motion. We can also teach you specific exercises to improve flexibility and relieve pain. Common in athletes like tennis players or golfers, and also for labourers like carpenters or bricklayers, shoulder pain is usually caused by overuse, strain, or injury. It can also lead to neck and upper back pain. Leg pain can affect people of all ages and from all walks of life. If your mobility is suffering as a result of debilitating leg pain, get in touch with our team of physios. We’ll provide the fastest solution to get you out of pain!
