PAEDIATRICS AND NEONATOLOGY

We understand the worry and heartache that surrounds the hospitalisation of a child. This is why we have worked hard to create a child-friendly environment to speed up the recovery process of our youngest patients. Our Paediatric Unit has the ability to treat a wide spectrum of paediatric diseases and conditions, from skin, eyes, ear, nose and throat to bones and joints.

What sets us apart

  • Dedicated wards for children
  • 24 hours dedicated team and paediatrician
  • Child friendly and experienced nurses who has been in the industry for more than 20 years

Our treatment and services

  • Immunisation
  • Eczema
  • Common cough and cold
  • Emotional and behavioral disorders
  • Infectious diseases

Neonatal

Our highly skilled and compassionate neonatologists, paediatricians, neonatal nurse practitioners, paediatric residents, nurses, respiratory therapists, and other team members provide care for all infants and families, including 24-hour in-house coverage.
