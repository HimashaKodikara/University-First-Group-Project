Dental Clinic

What is SWISS Dental Clinic

SWISS Dental Clinic provides a wide range of family dental care services in Sri Lanka to keep you smiling. Our pristine dental facility houses state-of-the-art technology and a first-class dental team.. Western Dental Clinic aims is to provide you with a dedicated, preventive, long-term approach in order to keep your teeth and gums in an excellent condition, and keep complications to a minimum. We limit the number of patients that we treat each day, so that you can be seen on time for your appointment in a relaxed and unhurried atmosphere.

Types of services offered at Dental Clinic?

There are the services offered at the Western dental clinic

  • Dental surgery
  • Braces
  • Implants/ Crowns
  • Dental Filling
  • Orthodontics
  • Whitening
  • Root Canal
  • Dentures
  • Dental X-Ray
