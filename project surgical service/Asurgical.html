Surgical Service

WH is equipped with operation theaters with facilities related to post transplantation care and emergency treatment. Patient safety infection control and continuous patient management makes WH surgical services a trusted choice.

Post transplant ICU facility

Sri Lanka's largest type of facility for post transplant care is at WH. Post transplant surgery patients are put in this facility, which is a hygienically safe environment with specialized doctors and nurses continuously monitoring patient activity and always on call if any emergency occurs

Medical Intensive Care Unit(MICU)

MICU provides acute care for wide variety of complex health problems including lung problems, kidney problems, liver problems, gastrointestinal problems, diseases of the blood, cancer problems and blood infections The multidisciplinary staff is highly collaborative and supportive, working together to achieve the best patient outcomes possible in an emergency
